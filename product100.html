No. 100 Series

Saima No. 100 Series Staples are built for industrial-grade strength and long-lasting performance. Made from robust 16-gauge galvanized wire, they support leg lengths ranging from 20 mm to 50 mm allowing deeper penetration and secure fastening into thick materials such as plywood, insulation, and hardwood framing. These staples feature a 10.8 mm crown width to distribute pressure evenly, which helps to avoid splitting or tearing, even in tough substrates.


Additionally, their sharp chisel-point tips facilitate smooth, reliable driving into materials without bending, making them well-suited for tasks like flooring installation, roofing underlayment, crate assembly, furniture framing, and door/window joinery. .The zinc‑plated electro‑galvanized finish provides excellent rust resistance which ensures durability in moisture-prone or outdoor environments.
