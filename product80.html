No. 80 Series

Reliable fastening
Available in leg lengths ranging from 6 mm to 25 mm and featuring a wide 12.8 mm crown, these 80 Series staples adapt seamlessly to materials from thin fabric to medium-density boards by ensuring strong holding capacity and even pressure distribution across surfaces.
Applications
These heavy-duty 80 Series staples are ideal for securely attaching durable upholstery fabrics and canvas to furniture frames, caravan or boat seats, and cushions—providing firm hold for trim and padding work—and equally effective for fastening veneers, mouldings, picture frame backings, and light wood panels in furniture and kitchen-fitment projects, while also being frequently used in exhibition stand setups, shopfitting installations, and stage or set design to affix decorative boards, fabric backdrops, and display elements.
