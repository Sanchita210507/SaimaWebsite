No. 10 Staples

Saima No. 10 Staples are precision-engineered office staples built for reliability, accuracy, and ease of use. With sharp tips for effortless insertion, No. 10 staples ensures smooth and consistent stapling performance across all applications.The compact leg length design offers flexible usability for stapling thin stacks of documents—such as reports, calendars or notebooks without tearing or over-penetration.Additionally, the uniform electro-galvanized coating provides consistent corrosion protection across every staple, ensuring long-term durability.


Each pack includes 20 boxes of 1,000 staples each guaranteeing a consistent high-capacity supply that keeps your workflow seamless and efficient.Saima No. 10 Staples are a trusted choice for clean and efficient document fastening in office, educational, and home environments, and are ideal for typical paperwork duties like binding 10–20 sheets neatly.
