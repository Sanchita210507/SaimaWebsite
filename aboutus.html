About Us

About Industry

Venkatesh Industries is one of the renowned manufacturing industries.Established in 2010 we are specialized in the procurement of CO2 MIG welding wire. In our pursuit of excellence and specialization, we launched our dedicated brand Saima Staples in 2022 focused exclusively on manufacturing high-performance industrial and office staple pins. Each staple is manufactured using premium raw materials and undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure flawless performance.

Our Mission

Saima Staples is committed to excellence and innovation, prioritizing quality in every product we offer. We are dedicated to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, with a strong focus on sustainability and eco-friendly practices to create lasting value for our customers and the environment.Our mission is to empower industries and businesses by providing durable, efficient, and consistent stapling solutions that enhance productivity and support seamless operations.

Our Vision

Our vision is to drive continuous innovation in our products and processes, anticipating industry trends and consistently exceeding customer expectations. Saima Staples aspires to set new benchmarks in quality, performance, and customer-centric practices within the stapling industry. The organization envisions becoming a global leader in the manufacturing of office and industrial staple pins, with a steadfast commitment to adding long-term value for its stakeholders.

About Saima Staples

Saima Staples is a leading manufacturer of premium quality office and industrial staples. With years of experience and a strong focus on quality, we deliver staple pins that are durable, reliable, and engineered to perform at the highest standards.

Our manufacturing uses high-grade raw materials, automated systems, and strict quality checks to ensure consistency and precision. Whether for daily office work or heavy-duty industrial use, our products are built to deliver smooth, jam-free performance.

Backed by a modern infrastructure and driven by innovation, we are committed to timely delivery and long-term client satisfaction across India and beyond.

Our Leadership

Mr.Dhananjay Deshmukh, founder of Venkatesh Industries, has extensive experience in manufacturing, particularly in CO₂ welding wire and now staple pin production. He founded Saima Staples with a vision to deliver durable, affordable, and high-performing fastening solutions.His commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction continues to drive the company’s growth and industry leadership.

Carrying forward his vision, his son, Akshay Deshmukh, manages the company’s marketing and business development. A Mechanical Engineer with an MBA in Marketing and International Business, Akshay brings a data-driven and strategic approach to expanding the Saima Staples brand across markets.

Our Values

At Saima Staples, our core values shape every aspect of our operations. We are committed to delivering high-quality, precision-engineered staple pins that our customers can rely on. Customer satisfaction, ethical business practices, and transparency are at the heart of our approach. We believe in continuous innovation to improve our products and processes while ensuring dependable and timely service. Beyond business, we deeply value our people and community—we actively support the health, safety, and education of our employees and extend these efforts to those around us.We believe in long-term relationships by offering not only exceptional products but also trust, confidence, and peace of mind.In line with our vision for a sustainable future, we embrace responsible manufacturing practices, minimize waste, and adopt eco-friendly solutions across operations.
